Bachelor of Secondary Education Major in Islamic Studies

The Bachelor of Secondary Education Major in Islamic Studies (BSEd – Islamic Studies) is a four-year degree program in the Philippines that will prepare you in teaching Arabic and Islamic Studies subjects in high school.

Taking this as your major will introduce you to Islamic history and culture including the contributions of Muslims to modern civilization and the history of Muslims in the Philippines.

As an Islamic Studies major, you will be taking the same professional education courses presented in the Bachelor of Secondary Education overview along with a couple of other subjects such as:

  • Elementary and Intermediate Arabic
  • Science of Qur’an
  • Comparative Religion
  • Science of Qur’an
  • Fiqhul Ibadat

AB History graduates can find employment as any of the following:

  • Professional Historian
  • School Administrator
  • Museum Staff
  • Historical Researcher
  • Historical Consultant
  • Legal Aide
  • Diplomatic Service
  • Professor
