Bachelor of Science in Marine Engineering in the Philippines



The Bachelor of Science in Marine Engineering (BS MarE) is a five-year degree program in the Philippines that is concerned with the construction and development of marine vessels and their component parts. This may include anything from private vessels such as fishing boats and cruise ships to military vehicles such as submarines, battleships, and aircraft carriers.*

Some examples of the courses that you may take while enrolled in the BS MarE program include:

  • Machine Shop
  • Electro Technology
  • Industrial Chemistry
  • Marine Automation
  • Shipboard Personnel Management
  • Ships and Ship Routines and Construction
  • Instrumentation and Control
  • Introduction to Naval Architecture
  • Marine Power Plant
  • Marine Pollution and Prevention
  • Maritime Law
  • Auxilliary Machinery
  • Engine Room Watchkeeping
  • Basic Safety

Additionally, a one year seagoing service onboard an actual ship may be facilitated by your school in order to give you an opportunity to apply what you have learned in school in a professional setting.
