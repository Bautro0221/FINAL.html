Bachelor of Elementary Education Major in Preschool Education



The Bachelor of Elementary Education Major in Preschool Education (BEEd – Preschool Education) is a four-year degree program in the Philippines that will train you in teaching preschool children.

As a Preschool Education major, you will be taking the same education courses or subjects in the general BEEd program . However, instead of the content courses in BEEd, you will be taking specialization courses focusing on teaching preschoolers. This does not only include teaching them basic subjects such as reading and writing, but also integrating social interaction in their learning process as well.

Here are some examples of courses that you may take while enrolled in this program:

  • Preschool Curriculum
  • Observational Child Study
  • Organization and Management of Child Development Programs
  • Introduction to SPED
  • Classroom Management
  • Child and Adolescent Development
  • Facilitating Learning
  • Social Dimensions of Education
  • The Teaching Professions
  • Principles of Teaching
  • Assessment of Student Learning
  • Educational Technology
  • Curriculum Development
  • Developmental Reading
  • Field Technology
